Industrial Services

Aerial Industrial Photography

Toronto Skycam offers a professional aerial photography solutions catered to industrial needs demanded by: Hydro plants, pump stations, oil wells, refineries, gas plants, etc. We are capable of adding significant value in many industrial applications.

Equipment Inspection: Aerial photography is quickly becoming the method of choice for inspection of industrial equipment. Using high-resolution imagery, mounted on remotely controlled aircraft, we accomplish in minutes, operations that once included, multiple personnel, very high levels of risk, and consumed a lot of time.

The images from our aircraft can be live streamed to the ground in full HD, allowing a qualified technician to visually inspect equipment from all angles without ever leaving the ground. In addition, images can be saved and stored for future review and comparison. This simple yet technologically advanced solution saves time, money, and prevents tragic accidents from ever occurring. Our inspection services are useful for inspection of: power lines, hydro towers, communications equipment, hydro dams, pump stations, and more.
